Privacy Policy

Edumate is designed to work primarily in offline mode. Your documents are processed locally on your machine using models stored on your device. Uploaded files and generated outputs (summaries, flashcards, keywords) are not sent to any external servers by this application.

You are responsible for safeguarding the files you upload and any generated content. If you share or move the generated outputs, please ensure they do not contain sensitive or confidential information that should not be disclosed.

This project does not collect analytics or personal information by default. Any logs printed to the console are for debugging and performance monitoring on your local machine only.
